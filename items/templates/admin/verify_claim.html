Verification Guidelines

✅ Signs of Legitimate Claims

  • • Specific details only owner would know
  • • Mentions of serial numbers or unique identifiers
  • • Describes specific damage or wear patterns
  • • References personal content (photos, contacts)
  • • Consistent with item description

❌ Red Flags

  • • Vague or generic descriptions
  • • Information easily guessed from photos
  • • Contradicts original item description
  • • Multiple claims from same user
  • • Suspicious timing or behavior
