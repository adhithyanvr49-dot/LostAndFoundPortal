Help & Safety

Your safety is our priority. Learn how to use our platform securely.

Safety Guidelines

Meet in Public Places

Always arrange to meet in well-lit, public locations with other people around.

Bring a Friend

Consider bringing a trusted friend when meeting someone to exchange items.

Daytime Meetings

Schedule meetings during daylight hours when possible.

Trust Your Instincts

If something feels wrong, don't proceed with the meeting. Your safety comes first.

Verify Identity

Ask for additional proof of ownership before handing over valuable items.

No Personal Info

Don't share personal information like home address or financial details.

Report Issues

If you encounter any suspicious behavior, scams, or safety concerns:

Contact Admin

Use our messaging system to report issues to administrators immediately.

Document Evidence

Take screenshots of suspicious messages or behavior as evidence.

Emergency Contacts

For immediate safety concerns, contact local authorities directly.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I verify someone's identity?

Ask for additional photos of the item, receipts, or other proof of ownership. Our admin team also verifies all claims before approval.

What if someone asks for money?

Legitimate lost and found exchanges should not involve money. Report any requests for payment to our admin team immediately.

Can I cancel a claim?

Yes, you can cancel your claims from the Claim History page. However, once an admin approves a claim, the process cannot be reversed.

How long do items stay listed?

Items remain active until they are successfully claimed and verified, or until the owner marks them as resolved.
